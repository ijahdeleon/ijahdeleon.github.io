HI ABI

Given the deep cultural roots and unspoken customs among Filipinos, breaking away from tradition can be a challenging task. The issue of financial instability among Filipino families post-retirement is a prevalent concern, wherein the concept of "Utang na Loob" or "Retirement plan" takes center stage, leaving many unsure of how to proceed.

In this regard, a short animated film can be a significant step towards creating self-awareness among families about the various alternatives available for their retirement plan, apart from relying solely on their children for financial support. As an effective tool for reaching out to audiences of all ages, animated films are deemed family-friendly and can capture the attention of both parents and their children alike. Furthermore, the films will explore both perspectives of the issue without disregarding any singular stance.
